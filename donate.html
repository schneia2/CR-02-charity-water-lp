Meet: Abel
Abel is an 8-year-old boy from rural Ethiopia. Just a few years ago, his days looked very different. Instead of going to school, he spent hours walking miles to collect dirty water—water that often made him and his family sick. But everything changed when his village got access to clean water, thanks to the support of people like you and the work of charity: water. Today, Abel is back in school. He dreams of becoming a teacher. He drinks clean water, plays with his friends, and no longer worries about getting sick from the very thing that’s meant to keep him alive. Clean water didn’t just change his day. It changed his future. You can do the same for another child like Abel.
